Upwork Automation Market Analysis: May-August 2025

üö® August 2025 Market Alert: First Contraction Detected

The automation market experienced its first decline in August 2025, contracting by -3.5% after three months of growth. This signals potential market saturation and increased competition. Strategic positioning is now critical for success.

Executive Summary

After three months of expansion, the Upwork automation market contracted in August 2025, declining from 6,205 to 5,990 platform jobs (-3.5%). n8n defied the trend with continued growth (+8.6%), while Zapier (-4.7%) and Make.com (-17.9%) experienced significant declines. Most notably, hourly rates jumped dramatically, with the highest reaching $999/hr and average rates increasing across all platforms.

Critical Market Shifts in August:

  • First Market Contraction: Overall decline of -3.5% signals maturation
  • Platform Divergence: n8n grows (+8.6%) while Make.com crashes (-17.9%)
  • Rate Explosion: Top rate reaches $999/hr; AI/LLM roles dominate premium tier
  • Application Bloodbath: Most apps decline 15-37%, only HubSpot grows (+18.9%)
  • AI Dominance: 7 of 10 highest-paying jobs involve AI/LLM integration
  • Multi-Platform Decline: Down to 18.6% from July's 20%
