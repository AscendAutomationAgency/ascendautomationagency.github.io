Upwork Automation Market Analysis: May-June 2025

Executive Summary

The automation job market on Upwork shows robust growth from May to June 2025, with n8n emerging as the fastest-growing platform (36% growth) despite Zapier maintaining the largest market share (51%). The overall market grew by 20.9%, with 4,256 jobs in May increasing to 5,143 in June.

Key Findings:

  • Platform Winner: n8n shows explosive 36% growth, suggesting increasing adoption
  • Rate Stability: Average hourly rates remain stable around $27-29/hour
  • Hot Applications: Shopify (40% growth) and Webflow (43.5% growth) are surging
  • Market Trend: Growing demand for multi-platform expertise (19.4% of jobs mention multiple platforms)
