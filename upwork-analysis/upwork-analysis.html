üö® September 2025 Critical Market Alert

The automation market declined sharply in September 2025, down 6.1% to 5,072 jobs‚Äîthe second consecutive month of decline. Total market is down 8.2% from July's peak. Executive Summary

The Upwork automation market experienced its second consecutive monthly decline in September 2025, falling from 5,402 to 5,072 jobs (-6.1%). From July's peak of 5,522 jobs, the market has declined 8.2% over two months. n8n demonstrates remarkable resilience with only -2.6% decline and now holds 41.4% of the market. Average hourly rates remain near $39/hr, suggesting quality over quantity continues.

Critical September Developments:

  • Accelerating Decline: -6.1% in September follows -2.2% in August
  • Platform Crisis: Zapier -16.2%, Make.com -11.6% month-over-month
  • n8n Resilience: Only -2.6% decline, maintaining growth momentum
  • Application Bloodbath: Most apps down 10-30%, only Salesforce growing
  • Rate Stability: Hourly rates remain elevated despite volume collapse
  • Market Bifurcation: Premium AI roles thriving while commodity work disappears
